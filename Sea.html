Capo Vaticano - Italy

Capo Vaticano is a great place for people who love the sea and quiet days. You can find many small spots between the rocks to sit and listen to the water. The views from the top of the hills are very good for taking photos of the Italian coast. It is a good place to go if you want to breathe fresh air and relax.

The weather is hot for a long time, so it is perfect for a summer holiday. Most days have a lot of sun and a very blue sky. The sea is very clear and you can see the fish in the water near the sand. If you like nature and calm beaches, this is the best place for your trip.
