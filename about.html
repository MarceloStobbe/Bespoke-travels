Beyond the Map

Traveling, for me, has always been about more than just visiting places; it’s about the stories we collect and the unique perspectives we gain along the way. I’m Marcelo Stobbe, and this space is a natural extension of that passion.

This isn't a commercial travel agency or a complex guide—it is a digital scrapbox of my personal journeys. I believe the best advice comes from real, lived experiences. Here, I’ve organized my favorite memories and honest insights from the places I’ve actually explored, from vibrant city centers to quiet mountain retreats. My goal is simple: to share authentic recommendations that might help you discover your own "bespoke" moments on your next adventure.
