Amsterdam - Netherlands

Amsterdam is a beautiful place where you can walk slowly along the old canals. The best way to see the city is by bicycle, just like the local people do. It is very fun to cross the many bridges and see the nice views of the streets.

The weather here is always special. Sometimes the air is fresh in the morning and sometimes the sun is warm in the afternoon, but the city always feels cozy. A short trip from the center takes you to the famous Keukenhof Park. Seeing millions of colorful flowers there is amazing and it is one of the best things to do in the Netherlands. It is a very simple and beautiful place to enjoy nature.
